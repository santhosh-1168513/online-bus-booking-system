Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?

To cancel your booking, click on the "Cancel Booking" button above. You'll see the cancellation charges and refund amount before confirming. Cancellation charges vary based on the time remaining before departure. The refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after cancellation approval. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. For UPI and bank transfers, it may take 3-5 business days. For credit/debit cards, it may take 7-10 business days depending on your bank.

How can I download my ticket?

Click on the "Download Ticket" button to get your e-ticket as a PDF. This is a paperless M-Ticket which you can show on your mobile phone during boarding. You don't need to take a printout. Make sure to carry a valid ID proof along with the ticket.

Can I reschedule my journey?

Yes, you can reschedule your journey by clicking the "Reschedule Journey" button. Rescheduling charges may apply depending on the operator's policy and time remaining before departure. You can change your travel date subject to seat availability on the new date.

What documents do I need to carry during travel?

You need to carry your e-ticket (either printed or on your mobile) and a valid government-issued photo ID proof such as Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License. The name on the ID should match the passenger name on the ticket.
